Why GOAT?

Greatest Of All Time — that's the bar we set for what goes in here. Not every PDF floating around the internet, but the ones that actually help. Curated, credited, and free.

The Vault

Not everything should be free to everyone immediately. The Vault is where the premium stuff lives — senior notes, advanced solution sets, exclusive guides. Access is by passkey, shared person-to-person. You earn it by getting involved with the community.

This isn't gatekeeping for gatekeeping's sake. It's about making sure the people who get the really good stuff are the ones who are actually trying to learn.

The Leaderboard

The community is what keeps this place alive. Every contributor who uploads notes gets credited — name on the page, name on the board, permanently. Contribute enough and you get Vault access.

Shoutout 📣

This site was designed and built with help from Claude (Anthropic). Coded the whole thing while I focused on the content. Respect to the AI, for real.

The Stack

Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No framework, no backend, no database. Hosted free on Netlify. The "CMS" is a JavaScript file you edit by hand — simplicity means it'll never break and never cost money.

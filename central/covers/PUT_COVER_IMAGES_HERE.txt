DROP YOUR BOOK COVER IMAGES HERE.

Naming: anything you like, e.g. smith-thermo.jpg, fogler.jpg

Then in catalog.js, set the cover field like this:
  cover: "covers/smith-thermo.jpg"

Supported formats: .jpg  .jpeg  .png  .webp

Tips:
- Square or portrait images work best (book cover shape)
- Keep file size under 200KB for fast loading
- If no image uploaded, emoji or fallback shows automatically

Alternative — use Open Library covers (no upload needed):
  cover: "https://covers.openlibrary.org/b/isbn/YOUR_ISBN-L.jpg"
  e.g.   "https://covers.openlibrary.org/b/isbn/9780073529264-L.jpg"
  Find the ISBN by googling: "book title ISBN"
